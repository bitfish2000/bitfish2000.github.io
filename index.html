Hi there, I’m Arsalan Zaidi

I’m a technologist and systems-oriented problem solver building solutions that actually work in the real world. I care deeply about open-source software/hardware, open data, and the craft of programming. I believe that technology - used responsibly - can be a means to solve wider social problems.

I use this site as a means to document some of the lessons I’ve learnt, as well as a place to sound out my thoughts. You’ll find posts here about tech, processes, people and education.
